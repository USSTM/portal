# Authorize Events through organizing Clubs

Each Event has one Owning Club and may have collaborating Organizing Clubs. Any identity authorized for an Organizing Club may edit Event details, while only the Owning Club or a USSTM Administrator may change ownership, change the organizer list, or permanently delete a future Event. Authority therefore follows current Club authorization rather than the individual who originally created the Event, enabling real collaboration without giving every collaborator lifecycle control.
