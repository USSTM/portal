# Browse and protect past Events

Type: AFK  
Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Keep past Events publicly searchable as historical records while preventing Club identities from changing or deleting them. Permit USSTM Administrators to correct or delete past records when required.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Public page and API can search/filter past Events separately from upcoming Events.
- [ ] Past ordering and pagination are deterministic.
- [ ] Club identities cannot edit or delete past Event through UI or direct API.
- [ ] USSTM Administrator can correct or permanently delete past Event.
- [ ] Administrator action is audited and public output updates accordingly.
